# General Creature Prompt Template

## Positive prompt

```
Create a completely original full-body 2D fantasy creature named [CREATURE NAME], a [SIZE] Riftling with a [BODY SHAPE] body structure. Its personality is [PERSONALITY]. It belongs to the [AFFINITY] affinity.

The creature has [HEAD FEATURES], [BODY FEATURES], [LIMB FEATURES], [TAIL OR REAR FEATURE], and [ELEMENTAL FEATURE].

Its dominant colors are [COLORS]. Add subtle [MARKINGS OR TEXTURES].

Pose the creature in a [POSE] stance with a clear, readable silhouette. Show the full body without cropping any ears, tail, wings, effects, feet, or accessories.

Use polished 2D game illustration with subtle pixel-art influence, clean outlines, soft layered shading, expressive eyes, premium fantasy-game rendering, and consistent lighting from the upper left.

Use a transparent background. No text. No border. No environment. No other creatures. No trainer. No logos.

The design must be completely original and must not resemble any existing copyrighted monster or game character.
```

## Profile extras

- 2048 × 2048 pixels  
- Three-quarter front view  
- Controlled elemental effects  
- Detailed eyes  

## Battle extras

- 512 × 512 per frame  
- Three-quarter side, opponent-facing  
- Consistent floor / float baseline  

## Overworld extras

- 128 × 128 pixel-art  
- Four directions  
- Major features only  

## Icon extras

- 256 × 256 head + upper body  
- Readable at 48px  

## Follow-up editing

Trim canvas · verify transparency · center subject · export WebP preview · update `asset-status.json` · never overwrite approved source
