# Ember Crater tiles & props

Volcanic top-down tileset: black rock, orange lava channels, ash, ember crystals, charred trees, stone bridges, forge. 32×32. Original Riftwilds only.

**Hazards:** lava, steam vents, heat shimmer overlay.

**Props:** cooling fountain, mining crystals, braziers, Ashmaw Colossus arena ring (marker), return portal.
