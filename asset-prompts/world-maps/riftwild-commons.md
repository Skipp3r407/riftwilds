# Riftwild Commons tiles & props

Create an original top-down fantasy hub tileset for Riftwild Commons: teal rift energy, stone paths, warm lanterns, dense forest border, crystal-powered buildings. 32×32 tiles. No text/logos.

**Ground:** plaza stone, path variants, grass, shallow pond water, farm soil, training dirt.

**Buildings (or facade props):** Hatchery egg pavilion, Arena gate arch, Marketplace stalls, Guild hall, Crafting workshop, Recovery center, Homestead shrine.

**Props:** Riftstone monument (glow), fountain, benches, lantern posts, notice board, training dummies, portal crystal (active + dim locked), berry bushes, fishing dock.

**Effects:** soft rift pulse, fireflies, light rain overlay.
