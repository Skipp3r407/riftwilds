# Pebblit

## 1. Creature name
Pebblit

## 2. Species title
River Golem Riftling

## 3. Personality
Stoic and dependable, speaking in slow rumbling gestures rather than haste. Pebblit protects what it cares about with unshakeable patience and surprising gentleness.

## 4. Primary affinity
Stone

## 5. Body structure
Biped golem — rounded river-rock torso, thick stubby legs, and oversized stone hands that hang low. Compact and top-heavy, like a pebble stack given life.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
A rounded boulder body with crystal eyes, massive blocky hands wider than its shoulders, and short pillar legs — a unmistakable pebble-golem silhouette, not a rock turtle or geodude copy.

## 7. Main colors
River gray, slate blue, moss-stone green, crystal cyan eyes, warm sandstone accent

## 8. Special features
- Body built from smooth water-worn river rocks fused together
- Oversized stone hands with visible sediment layers
- Crystal eyes set deep in the rock face like geode lenses
- Moss patches in rock crevice seams
- Small pebble toes that click when walking
