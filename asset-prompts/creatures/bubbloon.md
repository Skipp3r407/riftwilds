# Bubbloon

## 1. Creature name
Bubbloon

## 2. Species title
Bubble Drifter Riftling

## 3. Personality
Bubbly and easygoing, drifting through life with cheerful pops and gentle hums. Bubbloon treats every ripple as an invitation to play and bounces back quickly from setbacks.

## 4. Primary affinity
Tide

## 5. Body structure
Floating spherical amphibian — a round gelatinous body encased in a translucent water bubble, with tiny flipper limbs and no visible legs. Amphibious but not a frog or tadpole copy.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
A perfect floating orb inside a larger soap-bubble shell, with two small flipper nubs and a wispy tide-tail ribbon trailing below — instantly reads as "bubble floater" with zero frog silhouette.

## 7. Main colors
Crystal blue, seafoam teal, pearl white, deep tide navy, soft coral accent on cheeks

## 8. Special features
- Translucent water bubble membrane with refractive highlights
- Round gelatinous core body with large expressive tide eyes
- Tiny flipper limbs pressed against the bubble interior
- Wispy ribbon tail of flowing water trailing beneath the bubble
- Internal micro-bubbles that shift when the creature moves
