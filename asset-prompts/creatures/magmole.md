# Magmole

## 1. Creature name
Magmole

## 2. Species title
Obsidian Burrower Riftling

## 3. Personality
Industrious and stubborn, happiest when digging toward something worthwhile. Magmole snorts ember puffs when frustrated and purrs like rolling magma when content.

## 4. Primary affinity
Ember (primary), Stone (secondary)

## 5. Body structure
Quadruped burrower — long low body with massive obsidian claws on forepaws, compact hind legs, and a flat snout built for tunneling. Mole-like proportions but armored in stone and magma.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Low elongated body with oversized obsidian foreclaws, magma crack lines glowing along flanks, and a flat armored snout — a burrowing tank silhouette, not a diglett or mole Pokémon copy.

## 7. Main colors
Obsidian black, magma orange, stone gray, ember red crack glow, ash brown underbelly

## 8. Special features
- Massive obsidian foreclaws wider than the snout
- Magma crack lines glowing along stone-armored flanks
- Flat reinforced snout plate for burrowing
- Small heat vents on hind quarters releasing ember puffs
- Tiny blind-friendly eyes with ember glow behind stone lids
