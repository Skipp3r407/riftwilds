# Glimmermoth

## 1. Creature name
Glimmermoth

## 2. Species title
Luminous Mask Moth Riftling

## 3. Personality
Delicate and mysterious, fluttering between curiosity and caution. Glimmermoth is drawn to light but never blinded by it, offering gentle guidance through dark places.

## 4. Primary affinity
Spirit (primary), Radiant (secondary)

## 5. Body structure
Insect moth form — compact fuzzy body, six slender legs, and two large translucent wings. Mask-like glowing face plate distinct from real moth faces.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Oversized translucent wings framing a small body with a glowing mask face — a luminous moth silhouette with antennae like light filaments, not a butterfly or bat.

## 7. Main colors
Spirit violet, radiant gold, translucent pearl wing, soft lavender body fuzz, cyan mask glow

## 8. Special features
- Large translucent wings with radiant vein patterns and pearl shimmer
- Glowing mask face plate with expressive luminous eyes (not a skull mask)
- Antennae shaped like thin light filaments curving outward
- Fuzzy lavender body with spirit dust particles shedding from wings
- Delicate six legs with tiny glowing foot tips
