# Bloomble

## 1. Creature name
Bloomble

## 2. Species title
Seedling Sprout Riftling

## 3. Personality
Bright and eager, experiencing the world with fresh wonder every day. Bloomble grows slightly when encouraged and wilts only when truly lonely, never from brief setbacks.

## 4. Primary affinity
Grove

## 5. Body structure
Biped plant seedling — small upright body formed from a sprouting seed pod with petal ears and root feet. Clearly botanical, not a grass-type Pokémon or Bulbasaur copy.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Teardrop seed-pod torso with petal ears sprouting upward and root tendril feet anchoring below — a seedling sprout silhouette with no arms, only leaf nubs at the shoulders.

## 7. Main colors
Fresh sprout green, petal pink, seed brown, golden pollen accent, cream stem highlight

## 8. Special features
- Teardrop seed-pod torso with visible seed crack seam
- Petal ears that open and close like budding flowers
- Root tendril feet that curl and grip softly
- Small leaf nubs at shoulders instead of arms
- Pollen dust motes floating when happy
