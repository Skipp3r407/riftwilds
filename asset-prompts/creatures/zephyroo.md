# Zephyroo

## 1. Creature name
Zephyroo

## 2. Species title
Windbound Hopper Riftling

## 3. Personality
Free-spirited and restless, always scanning the horizon for the next gust. Zephyroo laughs easily, lands softly, and treats every hop as a small adventure.

## 4. Primary affinity
Storm

## 5. Body structure
Biped kangaroo-hopper — upright powerful spring legs, small forelimbs, and a long balancing tail. Avian-storm hybrid touches in the cloud fur, not a literal kangaroo.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Tall spring-loaded legs, cloud-puff fur crest, and wind ribbon trails streaming from wrists — an upward-leaping windbound silhouette distinct from any kangaroo or bird mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Storm white, sky cyan, electric violet accents, soft gray cloud fur, silver wind ribbon

## 8. Special features
- Oversized spring-coil legs built for powerful hops
- Cloud fur crest on head and shoulders like soft storm vapor
- Wind ribbons streaming from wrists and ankle cuffs
- Small wing-like feather tufts on forearms (not full wings)
- Large bright cyan eyes with wind-swirl pupils
