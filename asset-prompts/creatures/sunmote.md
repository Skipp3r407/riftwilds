# Sunmote

## 1. Creature name
Sunmote

## 2. Species title
Celestial Disk Riftling

## 3. Personality
Radiant and optimistic, spreading warmth without demanding attention. Sunmote hums soft melodies of light and circles friends like a living sunrise.

## 4. Primary affinity
Radiant

## 5. Body structure
Floating radial disc body — a flat circular core with ray-wing appendages arranged symmetrically around it, no limbs or head in traditional sense. Face is centered on the disc surface.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
A perfect circle with eight short ray-wings radiating outward and a soft halo glow — a sun-disk floater silhouette unlike any bird, star, or fairy mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Warm amber gold, celestial cream, coral orange rays, soft white glow, deep gold shadow

## 8. Special features
- Circular floating body with centered expressive radiant face
- Eight short ray-wings like soft petal rays around the disc edge
- Celestial halo glow ring hovering behind the body
- Surface markings like gentle sunspot swirls
- Particle motes orbiting slowly on a horizontal plane
