# Cindercub

## 1. Creature name
Cindercub

## 2. Species title
Coalspark Riftling

## 3. Personality
Curious and warm-hearted, with a playful streak that turns bold when protecting friends. Cindercub puffs small ember sparks when excited and curls into a sooty ball when shy.

## 4. Primary affinity
Ember

## 5. Body structure
Small quadruped with a compact, low-slung feline frame — broad chest, short sturdy legs, and a thick tapered tail. Not a house cat: the body reads more like a coal-briquette cub with segmented charcoal fur plates and rounded paws.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
A squat, blocky quadruped silhouette with a flame-tipped tail held in a gentle upward curve, glowing paw pads visible beneath, and two small horn nubs of cooled ash on the brow — reads instantly as "ember cub" without resembling any known mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Charcoal black, warm ember orange, molten amber, ash gray, soft cream underbelly

## 8. Special features
- Segmented coal-fur plates with faint crack-glow seams
- Glowing ember paw pads that leave tiny spark trails
- Flame-tail tip shaped like a candle flicker, not a bushy fire plume
- Ash horn nubs and warm amber eyes with ember pupils
- Sooty cheek tufts that smolder softly when happy
