# Astralynx

## 1. Creature name
Astralynx

## 2. Species title
Constellation Stalker Riftling

## 3. Personality
Regal and distant, yet fiercely devoted to those who earn its trust. Astralynx moves with starlit grace and watches the sky as if reading private maps only it can see.

## 4. Primary affinity
Void (primary), Radiant (secondary)

## 5. Body structure
Long quadruped stalker — lean lynx-like proportions but clearly cosmic, with elongated body, tufted cheek ruffs, and floating ear tips. Not a real lynx or Umbreon copy.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Sleek long quadruped with constellation dot markings, ear tips detached and floating above the head, and a cosmic tail trailing stardust — an astral predator silhouette distinct from any dark cat mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Midnight indigo, star white constellation dots, radiant gold connect lines, void purple shadow, cosmic cyan eye glow

## 8. Special features
- Constellation markings — star dots connected by thin radiant gold lines across the body
- Floating ear tips hovering above the head connected by faint void energy threads
- Cosmic tail trailing stardust and tiny orbiting star particles
- Large cosmic cyan eyes with star-field reflections
- Tufted cheek ruffs like nebula wisps
