# Noxling

## 1. Creature name
Noxling

## 2. Species title
Void Mark Riftling

## 3. Personality
Quiet and observant, comfortable in shadows and silence. Noxling reveals warmth only to those it trusts, dissolving into particles when startled and reforming with calm poise.

## 4. Primary affinity
Void

## 5. Body structure
Small biped imp-like creature — upright stance with long arms, short legs, and a tapered tail. Velvety skin and void markings; not a demon, imp, or dark-type Pokémon copy.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Upright biped with oversized void-glowing markings on velvety dark skin and a tail that dissolves into floating particles — a slender shadow imp silhouette with no horns or bat wings.

## 7. Main colors
Midnight navy, void violet, velvety charcoal, glowing magenta markings, soft silver eye shine

## 8. Special features
- Velvety dark skin absorbing light around edges
- Glowing void markings in geometric arc patterns across chest and limbs
- Particle-dissolving tail that breaks into floating void motes at the tip
- Large expressive silver-shine eyes in deep sockets
- Three-fingered hands with faint marking lines on palms
