# Frostuft

## 1. Creature name
Frostuft

## 2. Species title
Crystal Puff Riftling

## 3. Personality
Soft-spoken and cozy, preferring warmth in friendship over confrontation. Frostuft melts hearts with shy smiles and leaves tiny frost flowers wherever it naps.

## 4. Primary affinity
Frost

## 5. Body structure
Round quadruped lagomorph puffball — extremely fluffy spherical body on short legs, with no long ears. More cotton-ball hare than rabbit mascot.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
A near-perfect fluffy orb on stubby legs with icicle whiskers jutting sideways and crystal paw tips — a round snow-puff silhouette utterly unlike any ice fox or bunny mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Pale ice blue, soft cream white, crystal cyan, silver frost, faint lavender shadow

## 8. Special features
- Dense pale fur forming a spherical puff silhouette
- Icy whiskers like thin crystal needles extending from cheeks
- Crystal paws — translucent ice caps on each stubby foot
- Small round ears nearly hidden in fluff
- Breath visible as tiny frost curl when content
