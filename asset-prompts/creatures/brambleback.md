# Brambleback

## 1. Creature name
Brambleback

## 2. Species title
Thornplate Guardian Riftling

## 3. Personality
Steadfast and territorial, but deeply nurturing to grove life under its protection. Brambleback moves slowly by choice, never by limitation, and blooms when at peace.

## 4. Primary affinity
Grove (primary), Stone (secondary)

## 5. Body structure
Heavy quadruped armored beast — low wide body with bark-plated armor and stone segment plates along the back, like a living fortress tortoise without a shell dome.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Wide low quadruped with interlocking bark armor plates and wildflowers growing between stone back segments — a thornplate tank silhouette, not a turtle, ankylosaur, or bulbasaur copy.

## 7. Main colors
Bark brown, moss green, slate stone gray, wildflower coral and yellow, deep forest shadow

## 8. Special features
- Interlocking bark armor plates across flanks and legs
- Stone segment plates along spine with moss in gaps
- Wildflowers sprouting between stone plates on the back
- Broad flat head with stone brow ridge and gentle amber eyes
- Root-claw feet that grip earth firmly
