Floating spirit entity — upper body is a small rounded lantern-headed form with stubby arms; lower body dissolves into smoky wisps with no legs. Ethereal and vertical, not a ghost sheet or lamp copy.

A teardrop lantern head atop a tiny torso with smoky lower half trailing downward — a vertical flame-wisp silhouette with no feet, distinct from any ghost or lamp mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Soft cream lantern glow, spirit violet, pale gold, smoky charcoal, faint cyan rim light

- Floating collar of spirit energy around the neck seam
- Faint rune dots circling the lantern base
