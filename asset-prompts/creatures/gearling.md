# Gearling

## 1. Creature name
Gearling

## 2. Species title
Cogheart Construct Riftling

## 3. Personality
Methodical and earnest, fascinated by how things fit together. Gearling clicks and whirs when thinking and offers loyal companionship to those who respect its careful nature.

## 4. Primary affinity
Alloy

## 5. Body structure
Biped mech-organic hybrid — compact humanoid frame with visible mechanical joints, organic alloy skin panels, and rotating ear disks. Not a robot, android, or magnemite copy.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Compact upright biped with rotating disk ears, exposed energy core in chest, and organic-mechanical limb joints — a cogheart construct silhouette with rounded proportions, not angular mecha.

## 7. Main colors
Brushed bronze, alloy silver, electric cyan core glow, warm copper accents, soft gray organic panels

## 8. Special features
- Rotating ear disks with visible gear teeth on the rims
- Exposed energy core orb in chest behind glass panel
- Organic alloy skin panels with seam rivets at joints
- Three-fingered hands with articulated knuckle pistons
- Short stabilizer tail with a small spinning cog tip
