# Mossprig

## 1. Creature name
Mossprig

## 2. Species title
Verdant Antler Riftling

## 3. Personality
Gentle and patient, with quiet wisdom beyond its size. Mossprig listens more than it speaks, offering soft chirps and rustling leaf sounds when companions need comfort.

## 4. Primary affinity
Grove

## 5. Body structure
Slender quadruped cervine build — long legs, graceful neck, and a compact torso covered in living moss. Deer-like but clearly botanical, not a real deer.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Tall branching leaf antlers crown a narrow head; moss shoulders form a soft cape; a sprouting vine tail curls behind — an elegant forest sprite silhouette distinct from any deer mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Forest emerald, moss green, bark brown, golden leaf yellow, pale cream muzzle

## 8. Special features
- Leaf antlers with translucent golden veins that catch light
- Moss shoulders that grow tiny budding shoots seasonally
- Sprouting tail of coiled vine with two small leaf buds
- Bark-textured lower legs with root-like hoof tips
- Dewdrop eyes — pale green with crystal highlights
