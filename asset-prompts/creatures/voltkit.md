# Voltkit

## 1. Creature name
Voltkit

## 2. Species title
Static Weasel Riftling

## 3. Personality
Quick-witted and mischievous, always twitching with nervous energy. Voltkit zips between ideas faster than lightning and fidgets when forced to sit still.

## 4. Primary affinity
Storm

## 5. Body structure
Slender quadruped mustelid build — long body, short legs, elongated neck — built for darting bursts of speed. Weasel-like proportions but with storm-element anatomy, not a real animal copy.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Lightning-bolt ears rise from a sleek narrow head; a jagged static tail arcs behind a stretched low body — a darting zigzag silhouette unlike any fox or cat.

## 7. Main colors
Electric violet, storm gray, bright cyan, charcoal black, white static highlights

## 8. Special features
- Lightning-bolt shaped ears that crackle at the tips
- Static tail made of bristling charged fur forming a zigzag arc
- Storm-gray sleek fur with cyan vein lightning patterns along flanks
- Small paw pads that spark on contact
- Narrow glowing cyan eyes with vertical storm-slit pupils
