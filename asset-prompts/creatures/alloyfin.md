# Alloyfin

## 1. Creature name
Alloyfin

## 2. Species title
Bladefin Construct Riftling

## 3. Personality
Precise and curious, treating the world like a puzzle of currents and circuits. Alloyfin communicates in rhythmic clicks and graceful fin sweeps, loyal once trust is earned.

## 4. Primary affinity
Alloy (primary), Tide (secondary)

## 5. Body structure
Serpentine aquatic construct — elongated metallic body like a ribbon eel with blade-like fins, no limbs. Streamlined and horizontal, merging machine and fish anatomy.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
A sleek horizontal ribbon body with razor fin-blades along the spine and a glowing chest core — a blade-fin serpent silhouette distinct from any fish or robot mascot.

## 7. Main colors
Brushed steel silver, tide teal, electric cyan core glow, gunmetal gray, pearl white underbelly

## 8. Special features
- Metallic scale plates with brushed steel sheen and tide-teal seam lines
- Dorsal and ventral fin-blades like sharpened alloy sheets
- Glowing chest core orb pulsing with cyan energy
- Streamlined ribbon-eel length with articulated segment joints
- Propulsion vents along tail that release soft bubble particles
