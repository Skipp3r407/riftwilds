# Tiderune

## 1. Creature name
Tiderune

## 2. Species title
Pearl Serpent Riftling

## 3. Personality
Ancient and serene, speaking in ripples rather than words. Tiderune reads currents like scripture and guides lost travelers with fin-light runes.

## 4. Primary affinity
Tide (primary), Spirit (secondary)

## 5. Body structure
Serpentine aquatic — long flowing body with ribbon fins and no limbs. Eel-like grace with spirit rune markings and a pearl focal point on the forehead.

## 6. Unique silhouette description
Elongated S-curve serpent with glowing fin runes along the ribbon fins and a single pearl orb at the brow — an elegant tide-spirit snake silhouette, not a sea serpent or dragon copy.

## 7. Main colors
Deep tide navy, pearl white, spirit cyan rune glow, seafoam teal, silver fin edge

## 8. Special features
- Long serpentine body with smooth tide-navy scales
- Ribbon fins along body bearing glowing spirit rune symbols
- Single luminous pearl orb embedded near the forehead
- Spirit mist trailing from gill slits behind the jaw
- Large ancient calm eyes with pearl-white irises
