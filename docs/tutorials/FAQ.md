# Academy FAQ (source mirror)

Canonical searchable FAQ lives in `src/game/academy/faq.ts` and renders at `/academy?tab=faq`.

## Highlights

**Do I need SOL to play?**  
No. SOL is never required for basic gameplay (hatch, care, Live World, training, Academy). Credits are earned by playing.

**What are Keeper Credits?**  
Soft currency from play — not a profit promise.

**Are Arena Points cash?**  
No. Earn-only, non-transferable, no monetary value. No wagering.

**How do I move / talk / open map?**  
WASD (or arrows), E/Space to interact, M for map. F1 Help, F2 keybinds, Esc pause menu.

**Where is the Player Academy in Commons?**  
East of the plaza near the Rift Archive / training yard. Interact → Enter Academy. Archivist Solen can direct you.

**Does progress save?**  
Locally in `riftwilds-academy-progress-v1`. Backend sync later.

**Do videos autoplay?**  
No — off by default. Captions/speed/fullscreen supported when media is present.

For the full list, open the in-app FAQ or read `src/game/academy/faq.ts`.
