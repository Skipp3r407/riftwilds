## Art direction notes

- Palette: deep navy, cyan rift energy, amber accents (matches site tokens + logo)
- Creatures: original Riftlings — fox-like companions, not third-party IP
- Economy visuals: fantasy market stalls only — no Solana logos, no dollar/profit graphics
- Text overlays: captions/UI only; frames stay wordless where possible
- Honesty: cinematic enhancement of planned fantasy systems; not a claim that every system is fully shipped in alpha
