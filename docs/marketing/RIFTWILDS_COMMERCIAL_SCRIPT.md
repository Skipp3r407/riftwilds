# Riftwilds Commercial Scripts

**Product framing:** Browser fantasy multiplayer game — Riftlings, exploration, NPCs, quests, battles, crafting, housing, guilds, events, player economy.  
**Not:** A crypto investment ad. No guaranteed returns. No SOL profit promises.  
**CTA language:** “Play the alpha” / “Enter the Live World” / closed alpha — not “Play Now” as a public launch.

---

## Master narrative (all cuts)

1. **World** — floating islands, the Rift, wonder  
2. **Hatchery** — claim / wait with the egg  
3. **Hatch** — meet your Riftling  
4. **Live World** — explore, meet others  
5. **NPCs & quests** — talk, accept trails  
6. **Battles** — train the bond  
7. **Craft / home / guilds / market** — systems of belonging  
8. **End** — brand + alpha CTA
