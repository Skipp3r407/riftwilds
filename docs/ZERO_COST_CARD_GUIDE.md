# ZERO_COST_CARD_GUIDE.md

Player-facing guide — see design rules in [ZERO_COST_CARD_DESIGN.md](./ZERO_COST_CARD_DESIGN.md).

## How to use 0-cost cards

- **Glue:** Play after a 2-cost to still develop board on turn 1.
- **Not a win condition:** They chip and enable — they do not replace curve.
- **Deck limit:** At most **4** in a Standard deck.

### Example

Energy 2. Hand: Emberfox (2), Glowbug (0), Morning Dew (0).  
Play Emberfox (spend 2) → Glowbug (0) → Morning Dew (0). Legal; board + heal.
