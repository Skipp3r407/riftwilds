# Regional Stories

Each section matches Live World packs (`docs/world/REGION_CONTENT_MAP.md`) and named NPC casts. Quests/events named in packs stay canonical; prose expands theme.

## Riftwild Commons — Home Between Worlds

**Theme:** Lantern plaza, teal rift haze, refuge crossroads.  
**Riftstone:** Prime fragment; night maps of unfound places.  
**Story spine:** Founding refuge → Hatchery Compact practice → Present Awakening aurora.

**Beats:**

1. New Keepers learn care before portals (starter chain).
2. Plaza jobs and lantern restoration = civilization soft power.
3. Optional `first_rift_light` aurora choice (curiosity vs duty).
4. Campaign returns here between acts; Riftstone foreshadows finales.

**Key NPCs:** Elara, Rowan, Mira, Bram, Tessa, Solen, Orren, Nyla, Pip, Rook, ambient Cal Reed.  
**Sub-note:** Outer woods beginner foes = Fracture residue slime, not “evil pets.”

## Ember Crater — Heat That Remembers

**Theme:** Ash haze, lava bridges, forge orange.  
**Story:** Emberlands Heart-song never fully died; forges inherited cracked guild halls. Cinder Sage Malrec reads magma glass. Kael maps safe stone. Vessa judges Keepers by how they treat heat. Warden Pyra holds burned grove lines.

**Arc:** Prove warmth can temper without consuming; uncover Lattice slag-glyph; Cindercrag Basin as outer tutorial basin.

**Events:** Ashfall. **Jobs:** Bellows. **Tone:** Respect the caldera’s breath.

## Moonwater Coast — Charts of Two Moons

**Theme:** Moonlit fog, wet sand, lighthouse.  
**Story:** Tidehold Principalities once bargained with Hearts for weather. Fracture skewed shores; Tide Riftlings remember routes that no longer meet. Luma charts gates; Beacon Rock warns of tide-rifts.

**Arc:** Recover drowned Activation diary; choose law (Beacon) vs mercy-smuggling (Tidecutter). Tidefair event as cultural healing.

## Stoneheart Canyon — Law Written in Fossil
