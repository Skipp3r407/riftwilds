Expands About birth lore + `docs/PET_LORE.md` species system. Species short/standard/full bios remain in `src/content/pets/lore/`.

## Ontology (canon)

1. **Gateway Heart** releases living **fragments**.
2. Fragment **bonds** with surviving life/force/memory/machine.
3. **Egg** shell = crystallized Gateway energy + regional matter.
4. **Hatch** → living archive with dominant **affinity**.
5. **Keeper bond** organizes memory → personal **identity**.
6. **Evolution** = identity-driven transformation (About `whyPetsEvolve`).

Slogan remains binding: preservation first; combat culture later.

## Choice culture (etiquette)

From species lore refrain — elevate to world law under Hatchery Compact:

- You do not yank a Riftling into a bond. You **invite**, wait, keep the invitation honest.
- Forced bonding risks uncontrolled mutation and memory storm (villain Hex temptation).
- Breeding (`eggOriginSource: "BREEDING"`) may invent parents; wild origins must never invent them (existing generator rule).

## Eggs

Shell materials by region (About-aligned): ashglass, tide-salt, rootfiber, ozone lace, fossil grit, frostfilm, prismdust, echo-mist, machine filament, marshlight, starlit frost.

Eggs are **protected becoming**, not prisons. Inspecting (Mira’s lesson) is respect; smashing for fragments is Compact crime.

## Memory & dreams

Riftlings carry inherited feeling. Symptoms of unmanaged archive:

- Pull toward unstable Rifts
- Dreams of places that no longer exist
- Echo-speech (Spirit/Celestial lean)

Keepers help sort *inherited* vs *self*. Personal biographies (`backstory-generator`) are the micro-expression of this canon.
