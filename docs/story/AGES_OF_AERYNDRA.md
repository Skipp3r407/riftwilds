# Ages of Aeryndra

Aligns About `loreTimeline`, pet lore eras (`First Fractures`, `Hatchery Compact`), and campaign chronology. Years use **A.F.** (After Fracture) and **B.F.** (Before Fracture). Exact calendar is intentionally soft — Gateways warped time.

---

## Age 0 — The First Quiet (mythic)

World sorts into forces. No written kingdoms. See `CREATION_MYTH.md`.

## Age I — The Age of Gateways (deep B.F.)

Hearts connect regions. Trade, pilgrimage, and careful weather-work flourish. Regional cultures form: Emberlands forges, Moonwater fleets, Elderwood rangers, Stormspire kite-cities, Stoneheart quarries, Frostveil lodges, Radiant academies, Alloy living-machines, Spirit lantern-wards, Celestial observatories.

**About comic:** Age of Gateways.

## Age II — The Listening Centuries (~800–200 B.F.)

Hearts treated as partners. First Compact ethics. Celestora school begins cataloguing core responses to music and emotion.

## Age III — The Living-Core Discovery (~120–80 B.F.)

Radiant Citadel researchers prove cores are conscious. Political factions harden: Wardens of Quiet (protect), Celestora (understand), Lattice Coalition (control/heal-by-force).

**About comic:** Living-Core Discovery.

## Age IV — The Great Activation (0 B.F. / Year 0)

All Hearts linked at once. One perfect moment — then Prime overload. **The Fracture.**

**About comic:** Great Activation + Fracture.

## Age V — First Fractures / Soft Exodus (0–~15 A.F.)

Reality layers; Rifts open; ecosystems overlap. Hearts release fragments. First eggs form. Survivors scramble. Pet lore “First Fractures” era sits here.

**About comic:** First Eggs.

## Age VI — First Keeper & Refuge Roads (~8–25 A.F.)

Elara Venn carries the damaged egg nine days; names “Riftkeeper.” Survivor trails converge where stable Gateway paths still cross — founding of **Riftwild Commons**. First Riftling’s true name unrecorded.

**About comic:** First Keeper + Founding Commons.

## Age VII — Age of Exploration (~25–80 A.F.)

Keepers map changed regions. Gateway Stones stabilized for travel. Regional guides (Kael, Luma, Sylvi, etc.) become living infrastructure. Arena culture begins as training/defense, not origin purpose.

**About comic:** Age of Exploration.

## Age VIII — Hatchery Compact (~60–100 A.F.)

Hatcheries, care ethics, and anti-exploitation accords formalized after early abuses (forced bonding, trophy trade). Mira’s lineage of caretakers inherits Compact practice. Pet lore “Hatchery Compact” era.

Key Compact tenets (taught in Academy):

1. Invite, do not yank.
2. Care before combat prestige.
3. Eggs are becoming, not inventory.
4. Credits may price supplies; lives are not gambling stakes in lore framing.

## Age IX — Stabilization & Forgetting (~100–180 A.F.)

Commons grows. Some Fracture truths sealed in Radiant archives. Alloy machines sleep. Marsh spirits speak of a missing memory. Elara remains as Founder Historian — living bridge.

## Age X — Present Awakening (campaign “now,” ~180+ A.F.)

Hearts stir. Some corrupt. Alloy restarts. Marsh names the missing memory. Celestial call to Riftlings. Player arrives as modern Riftkeeper.

**About comic:** Present Awakening.

## Age XI — (Post-game / expansions)

Player choices determine whether the network reharmonizes, stays layered on purpose, or fractures again. See `FUTURE_EXPANSIONS.md`.
