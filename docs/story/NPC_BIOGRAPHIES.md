# Important NPC Biographies

## Commons core cast

### Elara Venn — Founder Historian / First Keeper

- **Canon:** About First Keeper + in-game historian at Riftstone.
- **Past:** Courier near Elderwood; nine days with damaged egg; refused titles; guided survivors to Commons.
- **Present:** Greets new Keepers with measured hope; authorizes first portals; distrusts political leaders (including well-meaning Conclave).
- **Longevity:** Bond-bleed with First Riftling slowed aging — she is living memory, not myth only.
- **Mystery:** Will not name the First Riftling; may hint it still walks.

### Keeper Rowan Vale — New-Player Guide

- Raised in Commons after settlements stabilized; remembers being lost on day one.
- Orients players; points to Elara; warm practical tone.
- Faction: `commons-keepers`.

### Mira Shellbright — Hatchery Caretaker

- Tends Commons nests; teaches first hatch/bond; shop for care supplies.
- Spiritual heir of Hatchery Compact practice (not necessarily bloodline of Compact signers).
- Soft-spoken; “quiet steps near the nests.”
- Story engine may call her for egg-sense advice (`first_rift_light`).

### Bram Ironroot — Commons Blacksmith

- Forge workshop; beginner tools; “good tools outlast loud courage.”
- Ties to Ember forge culture by trade, not loyalty to Hex.

### Tessa Windmere — Trading-Post Manager

- Fair-trade norms; reputation-sensitive shop (honest merchant).
- Embodies merchant_guild social values.

### Archivist Solen — Codex Keeper

- Fracture fragments catalogued; Academy/Codex bridge.
- Mentors students (incl. Cal Reed); tension with Serae’s redactions.
- Celestora-literate without being Conclave politician.

### Captain Orren — Guard Captain

- First combat as defense; commons_guard values (honor/town).
- Salutes heroes; challenges notoriety.

### Nyla Brook — Recovery Healer

- Temple/mercy lean; post-combat care; Compact medical ethics.

### Pip Gearwhistle — Inventor & Technician

- Map markers, portal readouts; Alloy-sympathetic conscience against Hex.
- Comic relief without undermining competence.

### Rook Emberfall — Arena Coordinator

- Frames arena as training/affinity control — aligns About “battle came later.”

### Cal Reed — Codex student (ambient)

- Studies under Solen; struggles with “page three” — player-relatable onboarding face.
- Future hook: can become junior archivist if player helps with Codex side tasks (additive).
