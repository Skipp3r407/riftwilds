# World History & Structure

## The world after naming

**Aeryndra** was the whole living network. After the Fracture, people call the playable layered lands the **Riftwilds** — not a new planet, but Aeryndra remembered crooked.

---

## Continents & realms (fitting the 12 regions)

Geography is post-Fracture: continents are **partially overlapping**. Travel uses portals, Gateway Stones, and dangerous peer trails.

### 1. Hearthmere Reach (fire–stone spine)

- **Ember Crater** (Emberlands heart) — caldera forges, lava bridges, Cinder Sage scholarship.
- **Stoneheart Canyon** — fossil shelves, quarry clans, mineral rites.
- **Cindercrag Basin** (sub-locale) — outer Ember ash basin used in seed quests; same cultural family as Crater.

### 2. Twinmoon Seaboard

- **Moonwater Coast** — twin-moon tides, beacon rock, pier villages.
- Historical realm: **Tidehold Principalities** (pre-Fracture fleets that bargained weather with Hearts).

### 3. The Verdant Deep

- **Elderwood Forest** — rootcrown temples, mosskeepers, spirit-moss trails.
- **Sproutfall Grove** (sub-locale) — fringe grove used for early keeper tutorials; still Elderwood culture.
- Pre-Fracture realm: **Rootcrown Concord** (ranger-councils, no single king).

### 4. Spirewind Heights

- **Stormspire Peaks** — kite-silk cities, wind trials, storm-guard beacons.
- Realm echo: **Spirewind League** (competing aerie-holds).

### 5. Auroral North

- **Frostveil Basin** — aurora cairns, ice bloom, warm lodges.
- Realm echo: **Stillwinter Holds**.

### 6. The Clarity Domain

- **Radiant Citadel** — healing gardens, archives, Living-Core discovery site.
- Pre-Fracture power: **Radiant Conclave** (scholars + healers + archivists). Home of **Celestora** manuscript tradition.

### 7. The Hollow Marches

- **Void Hollow** — null obelisks, portal labyrinths, hush cults vs sealers.
- Treated carefully in lore: Void is hush and hollow, not “evil by default.”

### 8. The Gearwild

- **Alloy Ruins** — salvagers, living machines, foundry courts.
- Pre-Fracture: **Alloy Compact** (builders of machines-that-grow).

### 9. Lantern Expanse

- **Spirit Marsh** — memory lanterns, whispering bog, shrine posts.
- Pre-Fracture: **Lanternfold** — caretakers of memories the living could not carry.

### 10. The Thin Sky

- **Celestial Rift** — floating islands, star anchors, observatories; campaign mystery locus.

### 11. The Crossroads (hub, not a continent)

- **Riftwild Commons** — built where stable Gateway paths still intersect. Refuge, hatchery, plaza, Riftstone.

## The Fracture’s political truth

About canon: a **coalition** of rulers, engineers, and scholars — not one dark lord — overlinked the Hearts believing they could end suffering. For one moment they were right. Then the Prime broke.

History books in the Citadel disagree on blame percentages. That disagreement is a campaign theme: **who gets to rewrite the Fracture.**

## Post-Fracture institutions

- **Riftkeepers** — vocation from Elara’s example; players are modern Keepers.
- **Commons Council** (soft) — plaza governance; Orren’s guards; Tessa’s trade norms.
- **Hatchery Compact** — care ethics (Age VIII).
- **Gateway Network** — Stones for discovery + fast travel (gameplay doc remains authoritative for fees/mechanics).
- **Guilds / factions** — see `FACTIONS.md`.

## Geography rules for writers

1. Do not invent a 13th launch region without expansion-pack process.
2. Sub-locales (Sproutfall, Cindercrag) are allowed as zones inside existing packs.
3. Continents may be referred to in books/codex; Live World still loads region maps.
4. Twin moons are Moonwater-canonical; other regions may see one moon through rift-skew — call that out as Fracture weather, not inconsistency.
