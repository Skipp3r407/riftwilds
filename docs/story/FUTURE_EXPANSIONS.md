## Expansion pillars (narrative)

### 1. Tidehold Deep

Sunken pre-Fracture capitals under skewed seas. Hook: Moonwater diary coordinates; twin-moon dark month.

### 2. Gearwild Crown

Alive capital of Alloy Compact above the Ruins’ fog. Hook: ARI-7 “remembers floors that aren’t on maps.”

### 3. Mirror Commons

A Commons reflected through a stable Rift — same faces, different Fracture choices. Hook: Riftstone night maps.

### 4. Second Moon Dark

When one of Moonwater’s moons goes dark for a season — global tide/rift modifier event pack.

### 5. Rootcrown Below

Under-Elderwood megamycorrhizal city of Grove memory. Hook: Elden’s “roots that answer in chorus.”

### 6. Spirewind Expanse

Open-sky continent of floating aeries beyond Stormspire. Hook: kite-silk messages from “above the last beacon.”

### 7. The Quiet Sea (Void-positive)

Ocean of hush where Void Riftlings thrive without villain framing. Hook: Veyra’s redeemed path.

### 8. Celestora Ascendant

Full Celestial campaign sequel: whether the Prime wants company or completion. Hook: post-credits map.

### 9. First Riftling Road

Optional expansion if mystery search is player-driven; never mandatory. Elara may refuse to join.

### 10. Lattice Fall / Lattice Mended

Branch expansion depending on `forced_lattice` vs `harmony_reweave` endings.
