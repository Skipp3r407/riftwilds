# Esports & Sponsored Circuits

## Rules

- Spectator **betting is never enabled**.
- Prize bands appear only when announced and funded.
- No pay-to-win entry that buys competitive card power.
- Collusion detection is required before high-stakes SOL pools.
- Skill determines winners; no gambling framing.

## Status

| Piece | Status |

## Exchange method

`esports` and `sponsorship` cards on `/exchange` link to Arena / Creator Hub until dedicated circuit pages exist.

See `docs/economy/TOURNAMENT_ECONOMY.md` and `docs/rift-arena.md`.
