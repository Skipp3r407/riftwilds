# Turn Structure

1. **START** — raise energy max, refill, ready units, start-of-turn effects, draw (skip for P1 on turn 1)
2. **MAIN** — play creatures/spells/equip, activate abilities, Commander power
3. **COMBAT** — declare ready attackers; resolve targets, reactions scaffold, damage
4. **SECOND MAIN** — Fast/Reaction plays and remaining summons (auto-skipped in Practice/Quick)
5. **END** — expire temp energy, end effects, pass turn

Actions: `PLAY_CARD`, `DECLARE_COMBAT`, `END_TURN`, `MULLIGAN`, `KEEP_HAND`, `SURRENDER`.
