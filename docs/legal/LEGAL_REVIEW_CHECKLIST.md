# Legal Review Checklist (SOL / real-money features)

**Do not publish legal claims or enable production SOL until counsel signs off.**

## Before enabling any SOL_* flag in production

- [ ] Consumer protection / refunds policy reviewed
- [ ] Regional restrictions mapped (gambling / crypto / lootbox rules)
- [ ] Age gating / parental consent where required
- [ ] Terms of Service updated for marketplace + tournaments
- [ ] Privacy policy covers wallet addresses + ledger retention
- [ ] Collectibles marketing: no investment / appreciation language
- [ ] Pack odds transparency meets jurisdiction requirements
- [ ] Creator IP / revenue share agreements
- [ ] Community funding / crowdfunding classification
- [ ] Tax reporting obligations for marketplace sellers
- [ ] Sanctions / geo-blocking obligations
- [ ] Incident response + dispute process documented
- [ ] Mainnet keys / treasury custody policy
- [ ] Penetration test of wallet + settlement paths

## Product copy rules

- Never promise profit, yield, or guaranteed value
- Never describe collectibles as investments
- Always disclose fees before purchase confirmation
