# FAQ.md (Rules)

Also see tutorials FAQ: [tutorials/FAQ.md](./tutorials/FAQ.md). Canonical: [RULEBOOK.md](./RULEBOOK.md) v2.1.0.

**Q: Why can’t I play this card?**  
A: Not enough Rift Energy, wrong phase, board full, or equipment with no ally.

**Q: What is a 0-cost card?**  
A: Tiny utility (Glowbug, Morning Dew, …). Max 4 per deck. Never big finishers.

**Q: Can I mulligan in Practice?**  
A: Yes — Keep / Partial / Full once at the start.

**Q: Why is turn-1 Energy still 2?**  
A: Raising it would speed the game mindlessly. Bricks are fixed via hand shaping + mulligan + curve tools.

**Q: Can I put food in my deck?**  
A: No — Inventory / Companion Care only.

**Q: Does Rift Spark count toward the 0-cost deck cap?**  
A: No — it is a match token, not a collectible deck card.
