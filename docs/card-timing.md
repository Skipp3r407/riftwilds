# Card Timing

## Spell speeds

| Speed | When playable |
|-------|----------------|
| Slow | Main phase (default for costly spells) |
| Fast | Main + Second Main (cheap tools / Rift Spark) |
| Reaction | Scaffold — nested during reaction windows |

Units are always Slow summons (Main or Second Main).

## Timing phrases (card text standard)

Prefer: When summoned · When played from hand · At the start of your turn · At the end of your turn · Before this unit attacks · After this unit deals damage · When this unit is defeated · Until the end of this turn · Until the start of your next turn.

Avoid: soon, sometimes, usually, nearby (unless adjacency defined), extra/stronger without numbers.
