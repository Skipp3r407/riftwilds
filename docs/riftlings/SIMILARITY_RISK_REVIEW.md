| Pokémon electric rodent / fire lizard starters | Aetherspark = sail-ear weasel + ribbon; Pyrekit = kiln-brick cub + chimney puff (no flame mane lizard) |

| Cute animal IP (Neopets etc.) | Regional crystal / rift accents required in prompts; original titles |
