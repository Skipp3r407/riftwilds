# Memorial Garden

Honors Hardcore (and memorialized) Riftlings. Normal play uses recovery instead of permanent loss.

## Memorial record

Name, species, level, bond, favorite food, achievements, battles won, obtained/lost dates, equipment, cause, messages, photos.

## Garden

Personal garden unlocks on first memorial: statues, flowers, candles, lanterns, benches, music, visitor notes.

## Social

Friends may visit, leave flowers/candles/messages via `/api/memorials`.

## Legendary Ancestors

Very old, high-bond Riftlings may ascend. Benefits are **lore / museum / titles / account cosmetics only — never combat power**.
