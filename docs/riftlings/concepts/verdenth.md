## 1. Creature name
Verdenth

## 2. Species title
Mossvein Keeper

## 3. Personality
Patient gardener temperament; hums when soil is healthy.

## 4. Primary affinity
Grove

## 5. Native region (design)
Elderwood Reach

## 6. Body structure
Stocky hexapod seedling-beast: four walk limbs + two pollen-fan arms; bark plate cape.

## 7. Unique silhouette description
Rounded moss mound with six limbs and a pollen-fan crest — not a bipedal leaf sprite.

## 8. Main colors
Forest emerald, bark brown, soft lichen silver, pollen gold

## 9. Special features
- Living moss veins that shift when cared for
- Pollen-fan crest (tool, not leaf hair)
- Seed-pod satchel on hip plate
- Soft root toes that grip bark

## 10. Signature ability (design)
Mossbind — temporary root-bridge for allies

## 11. Gameplay role
Care / support

## 12. Naming notes
Replaces Mosskin naming; distinct from Mossprig / Mossdrake
