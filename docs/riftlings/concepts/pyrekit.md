## 1. Creature name

## 2. Species title
Hearthcoal Cub

## 3. Personality
Warm-hearted daredevil; curls into a coal bun when shy.

## 4. Primary affinity
Ember

## 5. Native region (design)
Ember Crater

## 6. Body structure
Short quadruped with kiln-brick fur tiles and a chimney-tail puff (smoke ring, not flame mane).

## 7. Unique silhouette description
Brick-tiled cub with chimney puff tail — deliberately not a fire-lizard starter shape.

## 8. Main colors
Charcoal, kiln orange, ash cream, ember rose

## 9. Special features
- Kiln-brick fur tiles with cooling seams
- Chimney-tail that puffs rings when excited
- Glowing belly hearthstone (internal, not chest jewel)
- Soot whisker ticks

## 10. Signature ability (design)
Hearth Ring — warm ward that restores comfort

## 11. Gameplay role
Starter-adjacent Ember companion

## 12. Naming notes
Improved vs Embercub; distinct silhouette from Cindercub coal plates
