# Rift Storm Participation

## Qualifying actions

Quests, combat, bosses, gather, craft, fish, explore, public events, party help, heal/support, approved trading, Riftling care, puzzles, treasure, temp quests, region travel.

**Login alone does NOT qualify.**

## Scoring

Temporary per-storm score with base points + diminishing returns after repeated identical actions (`STORM_DIMINISHING_AFTER` / `STORM_DIMINISHING_DECAY`).

Examples: combat 1–3, quest objective 5, public event 10, boss participate 15, boss defeat 25, rare discovery 20, treasure 10.

## Fair selection factors

Verified score · streak tier boost · intensity · pity · recent rewards penalty · account age factor · fraud risk · randomness.

Not pure playtime. New players retain a chance (soft age floor, not a ban).

## Disconnect grace

`STORM_DISCONNECT_GRACE_MS` keeps score if the player reconnects in time.
