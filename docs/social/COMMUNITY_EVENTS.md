# Community Events

Mini live social events that draw keepers into hubs.

## Event kinds (stubs)

Traveling merchant · street musician · fireworks · town crier · pet parade · campfire story circle

Scheduled on hourly buckets via `listActiveCommunityEvents()`.

## Participation

Joining / attending grants Presence XP bonus at the event location. Requires engagement tier ≥ casually active and anti-AFK clear.

## Rewards

Soft only: Presence XP, tiny Credits / Community Tokens on participation checks (15–30m), cosmetics. **Never SOL.**
