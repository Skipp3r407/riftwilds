# Riftling Socialization

Companion ambient social behaviors in hubs.

## Behaviors

Sit beside owner · sleep near fire · play with toys · meet nearby Riftlings · sniff greetings · nap together · react to music · watch performers · pose for photos · …

## Traits

Shy · Playful · Curious · Calm · Energetic · Protective · Musical · Food-loving · Mischievous

Traits affect animation choice only — never disadvantage players.

## Rules

- Requires `allowRiftlingSocial` privacy
- No ownership / equipment control by others
- Lightweight sync; ends when owner leaves
- Soft bond gains daily-capped — **never combat power**
