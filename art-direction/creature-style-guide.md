# Riftling Creature Style Guide

## Required per species

Unique silhouette · recognizable head · one memorable feature (tail/horn/ear/wing/leaf/crystal/flame/energy) · 2–4 dominant colors · visible affinity cue · expressive eyes · clear personality · believable locomotion · readable at small sizes · profile · battle · overworld · silhouette · icon · idle · attack · damaged · happy care pose.

**Do not** recolor one body for different species. Avoid clutter.

## Body structures (vary across roster)

Quadruped · Biped · Floating · Serpentine · Avian · Plant · Golem · Spirit · Insect-inspired · Amphibious · Mechanical-organic  

Avoid making the full roster cats/dogs/foxes/dragons.

## Proportions

### Starters
- Head ~35–45% of height  
- Large eyes, compact body  
- One major feature  
- Friendly but capable posture  

### Evolved
- Mature proportions, stronger silhouette  
- Extra elemental features & markings  
- Confident posture  
- Clearly related to prior form  

## Canvas sizes

| Asset | Size | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Profile | 2048×2048 | Transparent, full body, padding |
| Collection card | 1024×1024 | Readable thumbnail |
| Battle frame | 512×512 | ¾ toward opponent, fixed floor |
| Overworld frame | 128×128 | 4 directions, simplified |
| Icon | 256×256 | Head + upper body |
| Silhouette | 512×512 | Solid dark shape |
| Evolution art | 2048×2048 | Dramatic energy, readable creature |

## Pose checklist

Full body visible · no cropped limbs · correct limb count · no duplicates · no text/watermark · transparent BG · affinity correct · lighting UL · strong silhouette · small-size test · family consistency · correct dims/name
