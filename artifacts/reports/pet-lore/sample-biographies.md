# Sample Personal Biographies

## cindercub

**First-Gen Cindercub**

Assigned as a starter companion egg in the Hatchery, chosen for a new Riftkeeper’s first bond. Since hatching, this Cindercub has shown a brave temperament: faces storms with ears forward. Ember warmth threads its memories—renewal after ash, courage that smells faintly of smoked fruit. Ember Crater’s cooling glass fields taught it to respect heat that can both shelter and scorch. Its story emphasizes daily courage: friendship, local paths, and talents that shine in ordinary hours. Its first memory was the smell of Emberberry drifting from a care tray—comfort before language. Unique habit: Arranges warm stones around its bed before sleeping. It fears the sound of collapsing cave walls. yet dreams to find the hidden passage it remembers only as a flicker of orange light. Shows loyalty in quiet ways—standing near, matching pace, offering warmth without fuss. The bond is new: curiosity outweighs certainty, and every routine is still a negotiation.

- Origin: Assigned as a starter companion egg in the Hatchery, chosen for a new Riftkeeper’s first bond.
- Habit: Arranges warm stones around its bed before sleeping.
- Mystery: It shares a recurring dream of a locked gate and a key shaped like its own pawprint.
