CORE STORY CONCEPT
==================================================

“Spark’s Journey” follows the newly hatched companion from Issue #1.

The egg rescued beneath the ancient Rift has now hatched into Spark, a rare young Rift companion whose energy pattern does not match any known element.

Spark is curious, powerful, frightened, and unstable.

The Keeper must escort Spark from Riftwild Commons to an ancient sanctuary where the companion’s true origin can be identified.

Along the way, the group encounters:

• A damaged region affected by the First Rift
• Travelers who fear Spark
• Hunters searching for the ancient egg
• A hidden passage only Spark can open
• Corrupted creatures attracted to Spark’s energy
• Evidence that Spark was created or protected by the ancient Keepers
• A dangerous choice between safety and truth

The issue ends with Spark choosing the Keeper voluntarily and revealing a new power that connects directly to the larger Rift mystery.
