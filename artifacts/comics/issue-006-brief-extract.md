CORE STORY CONCEPT
==================================================

“The Merchant’s Secret” reveals that the mysterious merchant network seen throughout earlier issues is far more than a trade organization.

The network controls hidden roads, black-market artifacts, companion transport routes, old Riftkeeper records, and access to sealed regions.

Its leader has spent years buying, stealing, rescuing, and cataloging objects connected to the First Rift.

Some believe the merchant is a profiteer.

Others believe the merchant has secretly kept dangerous relics away from the Veiled Meridian.

The truth is deliberately complicated.

The Keeper, Spark, and the companion team must enter a hidden moving bazaar known as the Gilded Crossroads to recover the stolen merchant ledger and identify who has been opening ancient doors for the Meridian.

Inside, they discover:

• Companions being traded illegally
• Legitimate rescue operations hidden among corrupt vendors
• Counterfeit Rift artifacts
• Real ancient keys
• Information brokers
• Smugglers
• Masked collectors
• Meridian buyers
• A hidden auction for a dormant Riftborn egg
• Evidence that the merchant leader knows the identity of the person who caused the First Rift
• Evidence that someone close to the Keeper’s allies has been feeding information to the network

• Mystery

• Moral ambiguity
