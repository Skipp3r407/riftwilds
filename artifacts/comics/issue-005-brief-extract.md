CORE STORY CONCEPT
==================================================

“The Storm King” follows the Keeper, Spark, and the companion team into a mountain kingdom hidden inside a permanent supernatural storm.

The region is ruled by an ancient monarch known as the Storm King.

The Storm King has survived since the First Rift through a bond with a colossal storm companion and the kingdom’s unstable Rift engine.

To outsiders, he appears to be a tyrant controlling the weather.

The truth is more complicated.

The storm has protected the kingdom from Rift corruption for generations.

But the engine is failing.

The king’s bond is breaking.

The storm is spreading beyond the mountains.

The Veiled Meridian intends to seize the kingdom’s core and use the Storm King as a living weapon.

The Keeper must determine whether to defeat the king, free him, restore his bond, or shut down the engine before the entire region is destroyed.

The issue should combine:

• Mountain survival
• Political tension
• Ancient royalty
• Weather magic
• Companion loyalty
• Aerial combat
• Rift-engine mystery
• Moral conflict
• Betrayal
• A massive storm battle
• A major reveal about the Meridian
