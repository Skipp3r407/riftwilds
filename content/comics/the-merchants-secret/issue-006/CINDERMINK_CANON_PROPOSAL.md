# Cindermink — Canon Proposal (Issue #6)

**Status:** Proposed → adopted for Issue #6  
**Origin:** Illegal auction holding pens, Gilded Crossroads  
**Role:** Assassin / Escape / Fire Utility

## Design lock
Small sleek companion; ember-red fur; soot-black paws; bright gold eyes; **chain scars around one hind leg**; smoke-like tail; nervous but defiant.

## Abilities (TCG-aligned)
| Slot | Name | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| Passive | **Cornered Flame** | Gain Speed when below half Health. |
| Active | **Smoke Slip** | Become untargetable until the next action or move to another position. |
| Ultimate | **Break the Chain** | Remove restraints from allied companions and deal Fire damage to controlling devices. |

## Story function
Emotional center of the rescue; Break the Chain frees captives during the auction battle; may travel with Mira’s team afterward as a free companion (never property).
