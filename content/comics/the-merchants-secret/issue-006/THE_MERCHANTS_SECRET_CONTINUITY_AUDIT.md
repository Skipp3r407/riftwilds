## Traitor cliffhanger selection

**Chosen: Cael Vesper (Lanternmaster).**

### Rationale (continuity-supported, not random)
1. Issue #6 brief explicitly: knows market customs, prior Merchant trades, **hides a previous agreement / may owe a debt**.
2. Across #3–#5 he alone maps Ember Route, Tempestria approaches, and circus sanctuary — the person who can sell “every door.”
3. Issue #3/#4 already established Lanternveil moral gray (moving Resonance Keepers “for protection”).
4. Nira is the Meridian-betraying hunter — casting her as the Issue #6 traitor would erase her #5 arc payoff.
5. Seris is an open antagonist — not a trusted ally reveal.
6. Page 25 transmission (“The Keeper has the egg. Proceed to the next gate.”) lands hardest if the receiver has been Mira’s closest adult ally.

Cliffhanger leaves motive ambiguous (debt / coerced / “protection”) for Issue #7 Traitor’s Gate — do **not** fully resolve here.
