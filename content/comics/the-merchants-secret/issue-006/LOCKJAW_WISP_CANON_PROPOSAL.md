# Lockjaw Wisp — Canon Proposal (Issue #6)

**Status:** Proposed → adopted for Issue #6  
**Bonded to:** Aurelia Voss (The Gilded Merchant)  
**Role:** Control / Utility / Information

## Design lock
Small feline-lizard companion; black fur with warm gold filigree markings; **keyhole-shaped pupils**; floating brass appraisal rings; silent gait; can phase through sealed doors when invited by contract.

## Abilities (TCG-aligned)
| Slot | Name | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| Passive | **Appraiser’s Eye** | Reveal one hidden property of a card, artifact, trap, or enemy effect each round. |
| Active | **Keyshift** | Move through one barrier or reposition one ally to a valid slot. |
| Ultimate | **Closed Contract** | Seal one enemy ability, portal, or artifact for a limited duration. |

## Story function
Detects counterfeit relics and false contracts; opens sealed archive doors for Mira under Aurelia’s terms; seals the auction vault during the escape.
